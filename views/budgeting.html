Budgeting Tips

1. Accommodation

Start with student accommodation when you first move in. Once you make friends, consider sharing a place outside, which is more budget-friendly.

2. Cook Your Own Food

Cooking your own meals saves a lot of money. Avoid eating out frequently.

3. Avoid Unnecessary Items

Only buy what you need. Avoid impulse purchases.

4. Track Your Expenses

Record your monthly spending. Use apps like Splitwise to split costs with friends when shopping or eating out.

5. Shop at Local Stores

Buy household items like cleaning supplies, laundry baskets, and wiping clothes from local, cheap stores.
